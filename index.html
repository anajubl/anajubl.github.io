About

Hi! My name is Ana Julia but I think if you are here you already know that.

I love to learn and to discover ways to make people's lives better and easier. I think that the technology is one of this tools but not the only one.

What I do?

I build beautiful and responsive (mobile friendly) websites using specially Wordpress and the best part is that I also apply some SEO and Analytics configuration on it.

Why I do that?

Because it doesn't matter that you have a beautiful website if your clients can't find it. That's why one of my priorities it`s build a website in a way that your clients can find it easily.

I already work with clients in Brazil and the United States. I develop websites for different kinds of business such as: dentist, lawyer, startup and in each one of them my goal is to understand what the client needs and build a website around that.
